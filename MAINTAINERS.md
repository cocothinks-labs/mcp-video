## Maintainer Checklist

- Keep `master` protected and releasable.
- Require CI before merging.
- Prefer squash or rebase merges consistently.
- Close stale generated-agent PRs when their fixes are superseded.
- Keep issue templates, discussion templates, and release notes current.
- Review dependency PRs in batches instead of letting them dominate the queue.

## Review Priorities

1. Security and FFmpeg injection safety.
2. Broken installs, imports, or package metadata.
3. MCP tool signature compatibility.
4. Test coverage for new public behavior.
5. Documentation accuracy and first-run clarity.
